Key Organisms

  • Bacteria: Two types, one thriving near hydrothermal vents, another feeding on the first type.
  • Tube Worms: Depend on bacteria as a food source.
  • Crabs: Predators that feed on tube worms.
  • Bioluminescent Organisms: Produce light by converting chemical compounds from deep-sea vents.

Environmental Conditions

The simulation accounts for harsh conditions, including:

  • Volcanic activity near hydrothermal vents
  • Water temperatures reaching 400°C near vents
  • Unique locations for organisms to thrive

Conclusions

  • Life in the oceanic world is well-adapted
  • Hydrothermal vents are vital for supporting life
  • The ecosystem demonstrates the importance of the food pyramid
  • New organisms should be designed to thrive near mineral-rich vents
